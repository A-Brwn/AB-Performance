Warm-Up Protocol

Complete before every session. Do not skip.

Asthma: Have your inhaler accessible. 2 puffs 10–15 min before exercise helps prevent symptoms.

1. General Warm-Up (5 min)

Cross Trainer / Bike / Treadmill
5 min
Light cardio — raise heart rate gently. Warm but not breathless.

2. Mobility & Rehab Activation (5 min)

Hip Flexor Stretch
2 × 30s each
Lunge position, drive hips forward. Tight hip flexors are common with desk work.
Glute Bridge
2 × 15
Drive hips up and squeeze. Activates glutes before all lower body work.
Band Pull-Aparts
2 × 15
Light band at chest height, pull apart. Rear delt activation before pressing.
Bodyweight Squat
2 × 10
Full depth. Controlled descent. Reinforces movement pattern.
Wrist Extensor Stretch
2 × 30s each
Arm straight, palm down, pull fingers toward you. Hold 30s. Tennis elbow rehab — do this daily.
Reverse Wrist Curl (Light DB)
2 × 15
Palm down, curl wrist up. Strengthens wrist extensors. 1–2kg only.

3. Daily Rehab (do every day, not just training days)

Child's Pose
1 × 60s
Kneel, sit back, reach arms forward. Decompresses the spine.
