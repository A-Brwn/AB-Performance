4-WEEK 1RM BOOSTER — BARBELL BENCH PRESS

Duration
4 Weeks
Sessions/Week
3
Primary Muscles
Chest, Triceps, Front Delts
Key Cue
Arch, brace, full leg drive, touch lower chest

This programme can be run alongside your existing training. Aim for 3 sessions per week with at least 48 hours rest between sessions working the same muscle groups.

Structure: Week 1 Accumulation (70–75%) → Week 2 Intensification (75–82%) → Week 3 Peaking (82–90%) → Week 4 Deload & 1RM Test.

Strength gains over 4 weeks are primarily neural — your nervous system learns to recruit more motor units and fire them harder and faster. The peaking structure maximises this adaptation.

Seated Cable Row 3 12 2–1–2 — Moderate — full range of motion — 90s RPE 7 Drive elbows back past the torso. Strong upper back = stable shoulder girdle = more force transfer on the bench. Retract shoulder blades fully at the end.

WEEK 4 · DAY 3

1RM TEST DAY 🎯

Deload is complete — your nervous system is fresh. Today you go for a new max.

Rest 3–4 min after warm-up singles. Rest 4–5 min between attempts. Pick weights you are confident about. Stop after 3 attempts regardless. A successful lift beats a missed PR.
