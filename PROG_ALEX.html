Warm-Up Protocol

5–10 min warm-up before every session. Adjust based on the day.

Legs Day

Bike / Treadmill
5 min
Light cardio to raise core temp. Bike preferred — warms quads directly.
Hip Flexor Stretch
2 × 30s each
Lunge position, drive hips forward. Essential before front squats.
Leg Swing
2 × 15 each
Front-to-back and lateral swings. Hip mobility before loading.
Empty Bar / Goblet Squat
2 × 10
Pattern the squat before load. Focus on depth and upright torso.
Ramp Sets
60% × 5, 75% × 3
Two warm-up sets before working sets on main compound.

Chest & Back Day

Band Pull-Aparts
2 × 20
Rear delt and rotator cuff activation before pressing.
Arm Circles
2 × 10 each
Shoulder mobility before pressing and pulling.
Light DB Press + Cable Pullover
1 × 15 each
Feel the movement patterns before working weight.

Shoulders & Arms Day

Face Pulls
2 × 20
Rotator cuff and rear delt activation before overhead pressing.
Lateral Raise (very light)
1 × 20
Blood into the delts before loading.
Light Curl + Pushdown
1 × 15 each
Elbow warm-up before heavy arm work.
