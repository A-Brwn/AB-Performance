Warm-Up Protocol

Complete before every session. Same warm-up you did in the first session.

Mobility & Activation (8–10 min)

BW Squats
2 × 10
Full depth. Controlled descent. Prepares knees and hips for loading.
Hip Openers
2 × 10 each
Standing hip circles or lying knee drops. Loosens hip flexors and external rotators before squats and lunges.
Band Pull-Aparts
2 × 15
Light band at chest height. Pull apart to full extension. Rear delt and rotator cuff activation before pressing.
Arm Circles
2 × 10 each direction
Large controlled circles. Shoulder mobility before any pressing or rowing.

Before Each Exercise

1 Warm-Up Set
× 10 reps
Do 1 light set at ~50% of your working weight before the first exercise of each muscle group. This is especially important on squats, RDL and bench press.
