Programme Guide

Which Programme Is Right For You?

Every programme includes personalised calorie and macro targets based on your stats and goal — whether you're trying to gain muscle, lose fat, or maintain.

All Programmes Include
  • Calorie & macro targets calibrated to your body
  • Auto-calculated training loads based on your stats
  • RPE tracking and progressive overload guidance
  • Coaching notes on every exercise
  • Lifetime access — one payment, no repeat charges
  • Syncs across all your devices via your account
🏋️
1RM Booster
Best for: hitting a new personal best on a specific lift

Your 1 rep max is primarily a neural adaptation — your nervous system learning to fire more motor units simultaneously rather than the muscle growing bigger. This programme peaks your nervous system over 4 weeks using percentage-based loading that builds toward a max test day.

✓ Flexible — runs 3 days/week and can be added on top of your current training with 48hrs rest between sessions targeting the same muscles.

💪
Hypertrophy
Best for: building muscle size and improving body composition

Muscle growth is driven by two primary mechanisms — mechanical tension (heavy loading through a full range) and metabolic stress (accumulation of work that creates the burn). This programme combines both across 6 weeks with three split options (3, 4, or 5 days/week) to fit your schedule.

✓ Choose your training frequency and the programme adapts — the more days, the more volume and the faster results.
