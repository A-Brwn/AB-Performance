Warm-Up Protocol

Complete before every session. Do not skip.

Asthma: Have your inhaler accessible. If you use a reliever, 2 puffs 10–15 min before exercise can prevent exercise-induced symptoms.

1. General Warm-Up (5 min)

Light Cardio
5 min
Brisk treadmill walk, stationary bike level 3, or cross-trainer on low resistance. Raise heart rate gently — warm but not breathless.

2. Mobility & Activation (5–7 min)

Hip Flexor Stretch
2 × 30s each
Lunge position, drive hips forward. Tight hip flexors are common with desk work and restrict lower body movement.
Glute Bridges
2 × 15
Drive hips up and squeeze at top. Activates glutes before all lower body work.
Band Pull-Aparts
2 × 15
Light band at chest height, pull apart to full extension. Activates rear delts before pressing/pulling. Elbow-safe with light band.
Arm Circles
2 × 10 each way
Large controlled circles. Shoulder mobility before pressing and rowing.
Bodyweight Squat
2 × 10
Full depth. Reinforces movement pattern.
Cat-Cow
2 × 10
Mobilises lumbar and thoracic spine — important when coming from a desk job.

3. Exercise Warm-Up Sets

Before each exercise
1–2 sets
Do 1 set at ~50% of working weight for 8–10 reps. For the first exercise: 2 warm-up sets (50% × 8, then 70% × 5).
